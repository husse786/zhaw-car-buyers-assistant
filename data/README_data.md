## Data Access

Raw datasets are too large to commit to this repository. They are available as follows:

- AutoScout24 car listings (tabular) — original source: <https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/clkmuhammed/autoscout24-car-listings-dataset>
- Car Damage Severity images — original source: <https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/prajwalbhamere/car-damage-severity-dataset>
- carknowledge.md (text) — included in this repository under `data/`.

Hosted copy of all datasets used (raw AutoScout24 CSV, cleaned autoscout_clean.csv, and the car-damage images):

- Google Drive: <https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1WjQEGJ5tCyqhf_GuT0Y04FDoLVM2QaTS?usp=share_link>
